Tile manufacturing flow — this calculator models the Press stage
Raw Material→ Ball Mill→ Spray Dryer→ Powder Storage→ Press→ Dryer→ Glazing→ Kiln→ Sorting→ Packing
Why the Press stage? The hydraulic press sets the base tempo for the whole line — its rated speed, combined with real-world stoppages and rejections, determines how many tiles the factory can actually deliver per day.
